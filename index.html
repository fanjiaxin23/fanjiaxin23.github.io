Fan Jiaxin (Prospective Ph.D. Applicant)

I am Fan Jiaxin, studying Master's in Transportation Engineering at Beijing Jiaotong University, China. I completed my Bachelor's in Transportation from Nanjing Agricultural University, China.

In my research, I want to use artificial intelligence technologies such as deep learning and Natural language processing to help urban rail transit emergency management. For this purpose, I aim to research that can automatically generate emergency assistance decisions, provide a reference for emergency management personnel, and thus reduce decision-making time. My current focus is to extract useful empirical knowledge from accident cases.
