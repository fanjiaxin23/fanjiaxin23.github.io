Fan Jiaxin (Prospective Ph.D. Applicant)

I am Fan Jiaxin, a Software Testing Engineer at the BYD Auto New Technology Research Institute, specializing in the reliability and safety of vehicle software systems. I hold a Master's degree in Transportation Planning and Management from Beijing Jiaotong University and a Bachelor's in Transportation from Nanjing Agricultural University.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Intelligent Traffic Systems (ITS), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Deep Learning, Knowledge Graphs, Data Analysis, and Visualization. I aim to leverage Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to develop automated decision-support systems for Urban Rail Transit (URT) emergencies.
